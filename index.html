About Me

Tinashe Nyabako is a highly motivated, results-oriented professional with 10+ years of experience at the intersection of Agricultural Engineering, Data Science, and ICT4D. He conceptualises, develops, and deploys technology-backed solutions, from AI/ML predictive models for food systems to water systems for climate resilience.

Tinashe has led multi-disciplinary projects across Southern Africa, funded by major donors including USAID, World Bank, GCF, and FAO. He currently serves as Chief Technology Officer at TINYA Pvt Ltd, where he leads AI/ML implementation and software architecture for client-specific business and agricultural solutions.

With an MPhil in ICT and Postharvest Science and a BSc Honours in Agricultural Engineering, Tinashe combines deep technical expertise with a passion for transforming complex data into actionable, sustainable outcomes.
